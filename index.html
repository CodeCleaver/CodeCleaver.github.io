The Story of Code Cleaver

My name is Jason and in 2021 I decided to learn to code. As I began my journey into the complex world of programming I found myself constantly scouring the internet for answers to my questions:


"How do I know I am on the right learning path?" "When can I start making money coding?" "Which coding language is right for me?"


Just a few of the questions you may have found yourself asking. I found that the more I learnt the more I needed to know and before I knew it I often found myself spending more time searching for these answers than writing code! That is why I have set out to eliminate all that wasted time trawling through the incorrect information and get you straight to the right answer.
